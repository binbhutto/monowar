    Cyber Analytics and Learning Group [ADSlab]

    Welcome to this Group of Autonomous Distributed Systems Lab, addressing the security-aware machine learning and optimization for AI-enabled distributed systems to adapt with the risk that operates in complex and real-world environments.

    The mission of this dimension is to advance the state-of-the-art of distributed machine learning, anomaly detection, machine learning perspective optimization, systems security, distributed computing for software-defined infrastructures. Currently, we are interested in the following five different problem areas.

    • Machine learning
    • Anomaly detection
    • Systems Security
    • Trustworthy AI
    • Machine learning perspective optimization

    Currently, this part of lab consists of eight Ph.D. students and one postdoc, and we are always looking for new excellent applicants. We welcome master’s and undergraduate students to join our group. If you are at UmU, drop me a line to set up a meeting. If not at UmU, look at our openings and opportunities as intern/vistor.

    ADS Lab is led by Professor Erik Elmroth where I am one of the research leaders. For any inquires, email at monowar@cs.umu.se
