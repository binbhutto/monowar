Autonomous Distributed Systems Lab

          [Cyber Analytics and Learning Wing]


Welcome to this part of Autonomous Distributed Systems Lab, addressing the security and privacy-aware machine learning and optimization for AI-enabled distributed systems to adapt with the risk that operates in complex, real-world environments.

The mission of this dimension is to advance the state-of-the-art of distributed machine learning, anomaly detection, machine learning perspective optimization, security and privacy, distributed computing for software-defined infrastructures. Currently, we are interested in the following six different problem areas.

  • Data synthesizing

  • Distributed machine learning

  • Anomaly detection

  • Security and privacy

  • Trustworthy AI

  • Machine learning perspective optimization
Currently, this part of lab consists of seven Ph.D. students and one postdoc, and we are always looking for new excellent applicants. We welcome master’s and undergraduate students to join our group. If you are at UmU, drop me a line to set up a meeting. If not at UmU, look at our openings and opportunities as intern/vistor.

ADS Lab is led by Professor Erik Elmroth where I am one of the research leaders. For any inquires, email at monowar@cs.umu.se
